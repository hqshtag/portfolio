In the summer of 2019 I learnt about Autofy on a internship website. I typically reached them out and after going through the selection process with other candidates. I was selected as their next Front end developer.

Autofy is a Tunisia startup specializing in the development of innovative IT solutions that facilitates the lives of people in Tunisia in the first place and then Africa. As a kickstart, the first project was the development of a platform that ficilitates used cars trading. My mission was to build and develop the Client side and the User Interface from the provided Mockups.
